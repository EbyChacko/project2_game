How to play TIC-TAC-TOE ???

Tic Tac Toe is a simple and fun game that can be played by anyone. Here are the steps to play Tic Tac Toe clearly with pictures:

  1. Open Edu-Fun-Zone page and choose TIC-TAC-TOE.
  2. Add Two Player Details.
  3. The player1 will have the icon "X" and the player two will have the icon "O".
  4. Click the "START" button.
  5. Click the "TOSS" button to choose the first player from the above two players.
  6. The player who win the toss will choose by the computer and will show in the notification area. And he/ she will play first.
  7. Players take turns placing their symbols until one player gets three in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  8. If all squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game is a tie.
  9. The game is over when one player gets three in a row or when the game is a tie.
  10. ENJOY The Game.

Remember, Tic Tac Toe is a zero-sum game, which means that if both players are playing their best, then neither player will win. However, it is still a fun and educational game to play.
